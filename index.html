About me

I'm Ferdy Irawan, a passionate and creative individual driven by a relentless curiosity for the world around me. I currently enrolled in University of Lampung, International Relationship studies, I have a passion in sustainable economy and graphic design. I thrive on the challenges that come with pushing boundaries and exploring new possibilities.

My journey in sustainable economic's area began . I am excited about the prospect of making a positive impact in global sustainable world and graphic design scop. If you're looking for someone who combines technical expertise with a passion for innovation, I believe I could be a valuable asset to your team. Thank you for taking the time to learn a bit about me. I look forward to the opportunity to discuss how my skills and experiences align with your needs.
